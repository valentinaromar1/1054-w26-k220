what the song means to me

that funny feeling is a song that mean a lot to me, because the song is just weridly comforting to listen to every now and again, the lyrics sets the tone of the tone well with what he talks about in the rest of the album

what the song is from is a netflix special called "INSIDE" one of his first time being in front of an audience since he stopped doing in person shows due to extreme anxiety on stage

the whole point of the show is the fact that the world is falling apart and yet everyone is stuck inside of their own problems and ignoring the outside issues just makes everything that your stuck with even worse

the song "that funny feeling" really sets a good story of how now matter how you try to distract from the problems, it will always come back to "that funny feeling" that comes with comprehending the fact that the problems are everywhere, and you cant ignore it forever, at least that what it means to me
